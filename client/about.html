About Us

Welcome to InternTracker, a comprehensive web application designed to enhance the internship management process for students and faculty. Internships are an essential part of academic and professional development, providing students with real-world experience and industry exposure. However, tracking multiple internships, maintaining records, and ensuring smooth communication between students and faculty can be a tedious task. InternTracker aims to simplify this by offering an intuitive and organized platform where students can log their internship details, upload relevant documents, and keep track of approvals and progress. Faculty members can efficiently review submissions, provide feedback, and maintain a structured database of student internships. The goal is to reduce administrative workload while ensuring students meet academic requirements. By digitizing the internship tracking process, InternTracker helps eliminate paperwork, minimizes miscommunication, and enhances the overall efficiency of managing internships within the department. The platform is built with a simple and responsive interface, ensuring that users can access it seamlessly across different devices. Whether you are a student trying to submit an internship report or a faculty member evaluating applications, InternTracker provides a smooth and hassle-free experience. This system brings transparency and accountability, ensuring that internship records are well-maintained and easily accessible for reference in the future.


InternTracker is specifically developed for the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at SSN College of Engineering. The department has a strong academic curriculum, emphasizing the importance of practical learning experiences through internships. Given the increasing number of students securing internships in various domains, it became essential to have a structured system to manage them efficiently. Before InternTracker, students had to manually submit internship reports, maintain spreadsheets, and follow up with faculty for approvals. This process was often cumbersome, leading to miscommunications and delays in evaluations. InternTracker resolves these challenges by providing a centralized platform where all internship-related information is stored in an organized manner. Students can easily submit details about their company, role, duration, and mentor, while faculty members can monitor and approve them seamlessly. The platform also ensures compliance with department policies, making it easier to verify if internships meet the required standards. By automating the tracking process, InternTracker saves time for both students and faculty, allowing them to focus on more important aspects of academic and career development. It acts as a bridge between students and faculty, facilitating smooth communication and efficient internship management.


The application has been developed by me, Shyam Sainarayanan Varadharajan, as part of my initiative to create a digital solution for internship tracking. I used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build this system, ensuring a lightweight yet powerful interface. The idea for this project stemmed from personal experiences and discussions with faculty members who faced difficulties in managing internship records manually. My goal was to develop an efficient, user-friendly, and scalable platform that would cater to the needs of both students and faculty. Throughout the development process, I focused on creating a clean design, seamless navigation, and secure data handling to enhance user experience. InternTracker is designed to be easily maintainable, allowing future enhancements to be integrated without major disruptions. I hope that InternTracker proves to be a valuable tool for the CSE department at SSN College of Engineering, helping students and faculty streamline their internship processes effectively.
